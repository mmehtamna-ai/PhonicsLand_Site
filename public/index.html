Meet Ruchi

R

Ruchi founded Phonics Land to help children build a genuine love of reading — through play, not worksheets or screens. She holds a B.E. and a Diploma in Early Childhood Care & Education, and has spent years teaching phonics one-on-one and in small groups.

Every family gets a personal connection — Ruchi checks in directly with parents and adapts her teaching to how each child learns best.
